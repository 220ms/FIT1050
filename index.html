CityScape Freerun

The afternoon started with a group of our member gathering, we then decided what we wanted to achieve in the session. The consensus was to practice and work on some vaulting techniques for getting across different wall heights. The different wall heights that we were attempting were 1 meter as well as 2 meters we practiced the Safety vault as most of the people that came along to the event were new to parkour and freerunning.
